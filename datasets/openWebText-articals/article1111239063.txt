In a city addressing unequal access to good jobs, Road to Hire provides a pathway to success for young people in Charlotte, NC. For Malik Robinson, his experience with the nonprofit led to a successful career as a front-end developer. But his journey didn’t end there — in September, Malik brought the skills and confidence he gained from Road to Hire to the set of a Bank of America production, giving him a chance to achieve his dream of becoming a professional photographer.
